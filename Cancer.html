CANCER

People that are associated with Cancer are very intuitive and sentimental. Cancer can be one of the most challenging zodiac signs to get to know. Cancers are very emotional and sensitive they care deeply about matters of the family and their home. Cancers are very sympathetic and they when they get attached to people they keep them close. They are also very loyal and able to empathize with other people's pain and suffering. This makes them a very caring person. As children they have to be approached with care and understanding. They are quick to help others and are quick to avoid conflict too. Thye are usually pacifists. When at peace with their life choices, Cancer show that they will be happy and content to be surrounded by a loving family and harmony in their home. Cancers are the people that care about family bonds. They like to keep strong long lasting bonds. They love to preserve family memories and keep them for as long as they can. Cancers grow up to be very good and caring parents.
