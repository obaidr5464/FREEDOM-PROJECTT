The Aries sign and its constellation represents a ram. Aries is a midsize constellation, in fact it is the 39th largest constellation. People can only see a few of its stars with the naked eye, to see the whole constellation you have to use a telescope. The constellation aries has different connotations in ancient cultures. The ancient Greeks and the ancient Egyptians linked the constellation to a ram. According to Greek mythology the ram was golden and was sacraficed to Zeus. The Aries constellation is located in the nothern hemisphere which is near where pisces is. The constellation was first classified by Ptolemy in the 2nd century.

The Leo sign represents a lion. This constellation is one of the earliest known constellations. The Mesopotamians named the constellation as a lion. Other civilizations called it other names but they all translated to a lion. The constellation is visible in the Northern Hemisphere during the spring equinox. In Greek Mythology there was a lion that heracules had killed. The lion was known to them as the king of all beasts. In the leo constellation Regulus is its brightest star, It is also known as alpha leonis. Another major star in leo is called Denebola, this star is the 2nd brightest star in the constellation. Denebola is 12 times more luminous than the sun and it has 75% more mass.

The cancer constellation is between Leo and Gemini. The shape of it is like an upside down Y. The cancer constellation is visible in the Northern Hemisphere in early spring. The cancer constellation represents a huge crab. According to greek mythology the crab attacked heracules. The crab had killed his family and heracules wanted to take revenge on the majestic beast. The major stars in the constellation are: Acubens, Al tarf, and Asellus Australis. The Al tark is the brightest star in the connotation. Cancer is the 31st largest constellation in the sky. It has an area of 506 square degrees.

The Pisces constellation is located in the northern sky. Pisces in latin means "The Fish". Pisces is one of the largest constellation and this constellation is also one of the constellations that were discovered by Ptolemy in the 2nd century. One of the symbols of Pisces are 2 fishes that are right next to each other one is upside down the other is facing upward and the other is facing downward. The Pisces constellation is on between Aries and Aquarius (Aries being on the east of Pisces and Aquarius being on the west side of Pisces). According to roman mythology the 2 fish represent Venus and Cupid who transformed themselves into fish to escape a monster named Typhon. Pisces is teh 14th largest constellation, it is 889 square degrees.The 2 major stars are the Eta Piscium and the Gamma Piscium. The brightest star in the constellation is the Eta Piscium and it is 316 times more luminous than the sun.

The scorpio constellation is located in the southern sky. This constellation was also discovered by Ptolemy during the 2nd century. The scorpio constellation is near the middle of the milky way so it is easy to see in the sky. Scorpio is the 33rd largest constellation it has an area of 497 square degrees. The constellation represents a scorpion. The scorpion is associated with Orion in greek mythology. The sumerians called it GIR-TAB which translates to "The scorpion". In greek mythology the scorpion killed Orion who was a mythical hunter. The 2 major stars in the constellation are Antares and Shaula. Antares is the brightest star in the constellation and Shaula was the 2nd brightest star in the constellation.

The taurus constellation is located in the norther sky. Taurus translates to "The Bull". The constellation is symolized by the bulls head. Taurus is the 17th largest constellation in the sky. It is has an area of 797 square degrees. The taurus constellation has been discovered since the early bronze age. The constellation has also been associated with the bull in many civilizations such as ancient greek mythology, ancient egypt and the ancient babylonians. In greek mythology Zeus transformed himself into a bull as a disguise to abduct Europa who was a princess. The 2 major stars in the constellation are aldebaran and elnath. Aldebaran is the brightest star within the constellation and elnath is the 2nd brightest star in the constellation.
