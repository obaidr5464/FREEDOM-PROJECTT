The Aries sign and its constellation represents a ram. Aries is a midsize constellation, in fact it is the 39th largest constellation. People can only see a few of its stars with the naked eye, to see the whole constellation you have to use a telescope. The constellation aries has different connotations in ancient cultures. The ancient Greeks and the ancient Egyptians linked the constellation to a ram. According to Greek mythology the ram was golden and was sacraficed to Zeus. The Aries constellation is located in the nothern hemisphere which is near where pisces is. The constellation was first classified by Ptolemy in the 2nd century.

The Leo sign represents a lion. This constellation is one of the earliest known constellations. The Mesopotamians named the constellation as a lion. Other civilizations called it other names but they all translated to a lion. The constellation is visible in the Northern Hemisphere during the spring equinox. In Greek Mythology there was a lion that heracles had killed. The lion was known to them as the king of all beasts. In the leo constellation Regulus is its brightest star, It is also known as alpha leonis. Another major star in leo is called Denebola, this star is the 2nd brightest star in the constellation. Denebola is 12 times more luminous than the sun and it has 75% more mass.
