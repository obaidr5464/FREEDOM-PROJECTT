SCORPIO

Scorpios are passionate and assertive people. They are determined and decisive, and are the type of people that search and workhard until they find out the truth. Scorpios are great leaders and are always aware of the situation and know how to deal with them. Emotions are very important for Scorpios they display them differently than other water signs. Scorpios will keep your secrets whatever they could be. Scorpios are known by their calm and chill behavior and by their mysterious appearance. Scorpios are fierce because they understand the rules of the universe ver well. Some Scorpios are excellent leaders because they are very dedicated to what they do. Scorpios despise dishonesty and can be very jealous and suspicious if you lie to them. Scorpios are brave and because of that they have a lot of friends.
